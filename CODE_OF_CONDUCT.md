# Code of Conduct for csvtojson-ts

## Introduction

csvtojson-ts is an open-source npm package that is maintained by Doga Budak. As a community, we value the contributions of all individuals and are committed to creating a safe and inclusive environment for everyone who participates in the project.

This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all members of the community and provides guidelines for behavior that we consider to be acceptable and unacceptable. We ask that all contributors to the project read and follow this Code of Conduct.

## Expected Behavior

We expect all members of the community to treat each other with respect and kindness. Specifically:

- Be welcoming and inclusive of all individuals, regardless of their background, identity, or experience level.
- Listen to and consider the perspectives and experiences of others.
- Communicate openly and honestly, while also being mindful of how your words and actions may be perceived by others.
- Be constructive and respectful in your feedback, criticism, and disagreements.
- Do not engage in any behavior that could be considered abusive, harassing, or discriminatory.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered to be unacceptable in our community:

- Discrimination, harassment, or abuse of any kind, including but not limited to sexism, racism, homophobia, and transphobia.
- Intimidation, bullying, or any other behavior that makes others feel unsafe or unwelcome.
- Offensive or derogatory comments or jokes.
- Unsolicited or inappropriate sexual attention or advances.
- Publishing others' private information, such as their physical or email addresses, without their explicit permission.
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional or community setting.

## Reporting and Enforcement

If you believe that someone has violated this Code of Conduct, or if you have experienced or witnessed any behavior that makes you feel uncomfortable, please contact Doga Budak at dogabudak@gmail.com. All reports will be kept confidential to the extent possible and will be investigated promptly.

We reserve the right to take any action we deem appropriate in response to any violations of this Code of Conduct, including but not limited to warning the offender, banning them from future participation in the project, and reporting them to appropriate authorities.

## Conclusion

We believe that everyone in our community has the right to feel safe and respected. By following this Code of Conduct and treating each other with kindness and respect, we can create a welcoming and inclusive environment that encourages collaboration and innovation. Thank you for your contributions to the csvtojson-ts project, and for helping us to build a strong and supportive community.
